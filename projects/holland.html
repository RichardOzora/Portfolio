Demo

The Holland Bakery project involved redesigning the user interface of the Holland Bakery website. This project was a collaborative effort with my teammates and focused solely on UI/UX design, created using Figma. It was assigned as part of our Agile Development course at university, where we implemented the Scrum methodology to organize and manage our workflow effectively. Through this project, we gained hands-on experience in team collaboration, agile practices, and designing a modern and user-friendly interface for a real-world brand.
